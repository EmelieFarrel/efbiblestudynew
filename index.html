About Emelie

A warm welcome from a young Bible explorer who loves Jesus and wants to help you discover amazing truths in God's Word!

Hello and welcome! My name is Emelie, I am 13-years old. I am so excited you're here! Whether you're just starting to learn about Jesus or you've known Him for years, there's always more to discover. Let's learn together!
